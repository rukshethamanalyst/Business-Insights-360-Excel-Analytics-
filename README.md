# Business-Insights-360-Excel-Analytics
__Project Overview-__
Business Insights 360 is an Excel analytics project developed for AtliQ Hardwares to analyze sales and financial performance through data modeling and reporting.

__The solution focuses on four key business areas:__

Customer Performance Analysis.

Market Performance Analysis.

Profit & Loss Analysis by Fiscal Year.

Profit & Loss Analysis by Fiscal Month.



__Business Problem-__
AtliQ Hardwares operates across multiple countries, markets, and customers. As the business expanded, management faced challenges in analyzing large volumes of sales data and measuring performance effectively.


__SALES REPORT:-__
1. Customer Performance Report-
      Purpose: Analyze customer-wise sales performance from 2019–2021,
      Role: Helps identify top customers, measure growth trends, and understand revenue contribution.

2. Market Performance vs Target Report-
      Purpose: Compare actual sales with target sales across markets,
      Role: Helps evaluate market performance and identify areas requiring improvement.


__FINANCE REPORT:-__

  1.P&L by Fiscal Year-
       Purpose: Analyze yearly revenue, COGS, Gross Margin, and GM%,
       Role: Helps assess business growth and profitability over time.
   
   
2.P&L by Fiscal Month-
       Purpose: Analyze monthly and quarterly financial performance,
       Role: Helps identify trends, seasonality, and performance fluctuations throughout the year.


__Technical Skills:-__

•Power Query (ETL): Cleaned, transformed, and prepared raw sales data for analysis by handling inconsistencies and structuring datasets.

•Data Modeling: Built relationships between multiple tables to create a structured model for accurate reporting and analysis.

•Power Pivot: Utilized Power Pivot to manage large datasets and perform advanced analytical calculations.

•DAX Measures: Created custom measures to calculate KPIs such as Net Sales, Gross Margin, GM%, and Growth %.
